Plant Care Tips

Welcome to FloraMart's plant care guide! Here are some essential tips for keeping your plants healthy and thriving.

Watering

Ensure your plants are watered regularly but avoid overwatering. Most plants prefer their soil to be slightly dry before re-watering.

Sunlight

Place your plants in spots with proper lighting. While some plants thrive in direct sunlight, others prefer shaded areas.

Fertilizing

Feed your plants with a balanced fertilizer once a month during the growing season for lush growth and vibrant foliage.

Seasonal Care

Understand your plant's needs during different seasons. Reduce watering in winter, and be prepared for pruning in spring.
